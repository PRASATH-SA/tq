Your Personalized Path

A roadmap to discovering and living your Ikigai.

1
Self-Discovery
Continue exploring what makes you come alive.
  • Keep a daily journal of flow activities
  • Experiment with new hobbies weekly
  • Reflect on past experiences that brought joy
2
Skill Development
Build on your natural strengths.
  • Identify and practice your core skills
  • Seek feedback from mentors
  • Take courses in areas of interest
3
World Connection
Find how your passions serve others.
  • Volunteer in causes you care about
  • Share your skills with your community
  • Look for problems you want to solve
4
Integration
Bring it all together into your daily life.
  • Align your work with your Ikigai
  • Create sustainable habits
  • Regularly reassess and adjust
